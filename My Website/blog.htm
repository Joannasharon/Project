Creating a website

September 30,2020

Why you need a personal website?

A personal website help people to have a visual representation of who you are. Since a personal website also includes your work experience and education, in this respect it's similar to a resume. Some even consider it a personal resume website. What sets a website apart from a resume is the fact that you only need to update it. You'll keep adding fresh things to your personal website - new projects, awards, skills, etc.

What to include in your personal website?

A personal website has a home page where you give an intro about yourself and futher more details like your skills and education in the about section. In the about page you can also add your work experience,projects taken and so on.. Include a contact page which has your contact information so that people can reach out to you.To make your website even more productive and effective you can write a professional blog which influence others by sharing your knowledge. You can use your own creativity and make people feel so engaged with your articles.
